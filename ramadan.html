DAILY RAMADAN ROUTINE

  • Pray Fajr and start by reciting Quran.
  • Start your day with studying your hardest subject.
  • Make a to-do list, to push you into completing more tasks!
  • Pray Duhr and Take a 15 min break!
  • Get back to studying ( Study your favorite subject so you don't feel drained ).
  • Take 30 minutes break and continue studying.
  • Pray Asr, and take a nap.
  • wake up before maghrib, and eat your lunch.
  • then go back to studying.
  • after praying ishaa, go to sleep to wake up early at Fajr and start the whole routine all over again!
